Risotto with salmon

Description

Risotto is a wonderful dish from italian cuisine. Creamy rice, along with a crispy, medium roasted salmon filet, that's a dish to die for.

Ingredients

  • risotto rice
  • shallots
  • white wine
  • salmon filet, skin on
  • vegetable or chicken stock
  • butter
  • Parmigiano Rigano
  • olive oil
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Finely chop the shallots and fry them with some olive oil in a pot over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the risotto and let that fry a bit while constantly stirring
  3. Add enough white wine until the risotto is covered and keep stirring
  4. Once the wine is reduced add a bit of stock, again till the risotto is covered and stirr until reduced. Keep repeating until the risotto is nice and creamy.
  5. In the meantime, scar the skin of the salmon, season it and roast it in a nonstick-pan with some olive oil skin side up for about 5 minutes.
  6. flip on the skin side and turn down the head. Let is slowly cook for about 10 minutes.
  7. Once the risotto is tender, stirr in some butter and grated Parmigiano.
  8. Plate, grate some extra Parmigiano on top, and enhoy!
